*Portabella Aioli**
*All herbs and spices are to taste. I did not measure these when making it.*

*Notes*

> **Ingredients** 
> - Portabella Mushrooms
> - Mayo
> - Horseradish
> - Garlic
> - Olive oil
> **Instructions** 
> - Saute mushrooms in a pan
> - Add mushrooms into a blender with garlic, horseradish, and mayo.
