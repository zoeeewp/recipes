**Basil Pesto**
*All herbs and spices are to taste. I did not measure these when making it.*

*Notes*

> **Ingredients** 
>    - Basil (Sweet Basil is preferred)
>    - Garlic
>    - Salt and Pepper
>    - Pine Nut
> **Instructions** 
> - Add all ingredidents into blender with basil, garlic, pine nut, salt, and pepper.
